Welcome to Achievement of Equality.
On this website, you will learn more about this important topic and it will give you access to take action to help end this problem. You will also have a game that will entertain the users and also inform them and give them more information about gender equality.

The gender equality game!

We built this game to show the importance of gender equality and how it's a huge problem around the world. In this game, you will experience a colored female that has to go through daily tasks and how it's different from others, it will also display the emotions she's going through at that moment. We felt like the mixture of cutscenes and the Mini-game was the best way to have the audience interact and also understand the problem. We hope you enjoy our game.

Created by Mohammed Hilfi and Albiona Uka.
